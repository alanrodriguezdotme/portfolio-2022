How we think

Our approach

As strategists, we’re advocates for consumers. We observe how people really live and think and behave. And our goal is to develop the “why we create” and the “how we make” in order to get to the “what we design”.


More than anything, we help turn research into insights. These inspire design that makes a difference in people’s lives. In small and not so small ways. For all kinds of people.


We partner with research and design, marketing and brand to ensure this point of view—what matters to people—lives on throughout the experience.

Workshops

Design thinking

Design thinking is a human-centered approach to innovation that integrates the needs of people, the possibilities of technology, and the requirements for business success. There are five pillars of design thinking—empathize, define, ideate, prototype, and test.

A creative strategist will lead your team through the design thinking process. Together we’ll uncover latent customer needs, and learn techniques to improve ideation and prototyping. This approach can help teams develop innovative solutions for the complex challenges our customers face.

 

Storytelling

Storytelling is the foundation of human understanding. Because stories are the best way for us to walk a mile in someone else’s shoes. To gain empathy for another person’s perspective. Their value lies in the emotional resonance they create, which motivates us to action. That’s why storytelling is the heart of design thinking.

In this workshop, experienced writers will help you discover how to tell a compelling story that engages your audience. A story that resonates.
