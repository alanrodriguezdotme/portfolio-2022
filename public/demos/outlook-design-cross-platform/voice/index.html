Workshops

Creative briefing

A good creative brief saves countless hours and resources, and leaves the energy for the creative work. Where it belongs.


Most importantly, a creative brief acts as inspiration, a compelling advertisement for the creative work ahead.


Using our one page creative brief template, we'll show you how to brief your projects, make your creative teams very happy to work with you, and ensure your projects are ultimately successful.

Voice

Like design, voice is undergoing a renaissance across the company. New philosophies, guidance, and style. What does this mean? For us? For our customers?


In our voice workshops, we’ll take a look at what others are doing, in our industry and outside. Then we'll look at some of our own work. And discuss how we might approach the same work in the future, using modern guidance.
